About

My name is Aminda Pereira, but I prefer to go by Mindy. Art is where
my passion lies. For the most part I typically draw traditional style, but I'm trying to break into
digital. I also enjoy playing the piano, I hope to one day incorporate the music I make in with the art
I create. I'm still learning, but that won't stop me from trying.
